Let nothing disturb you, let nothing frighten you, all things are passing away: God never changes. Patience obtains all things. Whoever has God lacks nothing; God alone suffices. Quiet your heart in this truth, knowing that He who cares for the lilies of the field watches over you.

The soul is like a castle made of a single diamond or very clear crystal, in which there are many rooms, just as in heaven there are many mansions. In the center is the room where the most secret things pass between God and the soul. To enter this castle, the gate is prayer and meditation.

Mental prayer is nothing else than an intimate sharing between friends; it means taking time frequently to be alone with Him who we know loves us. We do not need to speak many words, but rather to look upon Him with love, allowing His presence to warm our souls.

Christ has no body now but yours, no hands, no feet on earth but yours. Yours are the eyes through which he looks compassion on this world. Yours are the feet with which he walks to do good. Yours are the hands with which he blesses all the world.
