The more the Holy Spirit finds Mary, His dear and inseparable spouse, in any soul, the more active and mighty He becomes in producing Jesus Christ in that soul, and that soul in Jesus Christ. To belong to Mary is to be led directly and swiftly by the Holy Spirit to union with her Son.

The Rosary is a priceless treasure inspired by God. By reciting the Hail Mary, we greet the Mother of God with the very words the Archangel Gabriel used, keeping our hearts aligned with the saving mysteries of Christ. It is a powerful shield against temptations and a source of deep peace.

To know Jesus Christ incarnate, who is the Eternal Wisdom, is to know everything. To know everything else and not to know Him is to know nothing. Our whole perfection lies in knowing Him and loving Him, and this knowledge is best learned in the school of Mary, who knew Him best.

Mary is the safest, easiest, shortest, and most perfect way of approaching Jesus. Through her, we surrender our virtues, merits, and daily actions to Christ, allowing Him to fill us with His grace. By giving ourselves to her, we are stripped of self-love and clothed in Christ's charity.
