Our whole perfection consists in loving our most amiable God, and the perfection of love consists in unifying our will with His. The more we conform our desires to the divine will, the greater our peace and sanctity. In every circumstance, let us say: Thy will be done.

Acquire the habit of speaking to God as if you were alone with Him, familiarly and with confidence, as to your dearest friend. Share with Him your joys, your sorrows, your fears, and your hopes. He is never tired of listening to you, and His heart is always open to receive you.

He who prays is certainly saved. He who does not pray is certainly damned. All the elect are saved by prayer, and all the lost are lost because they neglected prayer. Prayer is the channel of all graces, and without it, we do not have the strength to resist temptation.

True peace is found only in conforming ourselves to the will of God in all things, both in prosperity and in adversity. When we accept whatever God sends us with a peaceful heart, we find a joy that the world can neither give nor take away. Conformity is the secret of the saints.
